CT500 5A
TECHNICAL INFORMATION

The CT500S 5A is a five axis CNC EDM hole drilling machine designed for production applications. With automatic electrode changing, extensive programming tools and Fast Servo and Advanced Break Through Detection, the CT500S 5A is one of the most advanced EDM drillers available today.

The powerful Siemens 840 Dsl controller and Current EDM’s comprehensive, drilling-specific programming provides the tools you need to produce consistent, high-quality parts.

To maximize yield, the worktable enclosure is open in the front for quick part loading and unloading. Safety light curtains guard the opening ensuring safe machine operation. Scheduled maintenance is simplified with easily accessible machine components. The CT500S is also available in three axis configuration.

STANDARD MACHINE FEATURES

Rigid FIXED BRIDGE design for optimum machining accuracy and low maintenance.

X/Y/Z axis travel of 22x14x16 inch (559x356x406mm) enables the drilling of large or multiple workpieces.

High-precision, recirculating ball way slides used on X, Y and Z axes. Precision ground ball screws for optimum positioning accuracy.

Powerful Siemens 840 DsI industrial controller for complex programming.

Specially designed power supply cabinet to shield and cool printed circuit boards and other internal components. Multiple power supply circuit breakers protect and extend the life of critical components.

One amp power setting for drilling with extremely small diameter electrodes. Reset Discharge (RD) energy control improves electrode referencing accuracy and simplifies the positioning of electrodes in existing holes or cavities.

"SOFT START" feature virtually eliminates entry point burr formation. Power settings are automatically lowered upon initial contact and then gradually increased to full power.

Automatic part referencing to reduce setup time.

Precision lapped GRANITE WORKTABLE. Exceptional flatness and electrical insulation enhance machining performance.

ELECTRICAL REQUIREMENTS

The CT500 requires a dedicated 400VAC, 3-phase power source rated at 5KVA. Voltage regulation is required if voltage fluctuates more than 5%. Connect machine ground directly to a copper ground rod.

ENVIRONMENTAL REQUIREMENTS

Optimum machine performance is achieved in the following environment:

  1. Ambient temperature: 70 degrees F (21 degrees C)
  2. Temperature variation less than 1.5 degrees F/hr. (0.6 degrees C/hr.)
  3. Placement of equipment away from floor vibrations, dust, mist and direct sunlight.
