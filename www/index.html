RT6050 5A
TECHNICAL INFORMATION

With an enormous work envelope and part capacity up to 1,200lbs. (545kg.), there is virtually no EDM drilling job the RT6050 can’t handle. With automatic electrode and guide changing, extensive programming tools and our Fast Servo and Advanced Break- Through Detection, the RT6050 is one of the most advanced EDM Drillers available today.

The moving gantry design allows easy part loading and ergonomic access for long, safe operation. The flex mount control arm accommodates a wide variety of operator running positions. In addition, the table side panels are removable for large, overhanging parts.

For long running jobs, the RT6050 can be equipped with a 48 position tool changer and 8 position guide changer. Machine lubrication is simple with two easily accessed grease manifolds. The RT6050 is also available in 3 Axis configuration.

Two new features greatly enhance the usability of this large machine. Hand Pendant Teach Mode Programming allows operator to “Teach” program locations into a file. Similar to the standard “Teach” mode on the control (MDA Mode), this feature will allow remote teaching from the hand pendant control (JOG Mode). Hand Pendant Rework allows the operator to manually drill a hole using the hand pendant. From the pendant, you can start and end a drilling cycle without having to go back to the main control unit.

CT400 3A
TECHNICAL INFORMATION

The CT400 3A is a three axis CNC EDM hole drilling machine designed for applications requiring holes at multiple angles. It features rugged construction and high-quality components throughout to ensure accuracy and consistency for years of continuous use. With automatic electrode changing, multiple axis positioning and extensive programming tools, the CT400 3A is one of the most versatile EDM drillers available.

The CT400 is also available in five axis configuration.

STANDARD MACHINE FEATURES

MOVING BRIDGE construction enables extended Y-axis stroke while minimizing machine footprint. The stationary worktable increases maximum part weight capacity.

High-precision, recirculating ball way slides used on X, Y and Z axes. Precision ball screws for optimum positioning accuracy.

Precision lapped GRANITE WORKTABLE. Exceptional flatness and electrical insulation enhance machining performance.

Siemens M Controller for complex, 3 axis programming. The optional Siemens 840DsI Controller is available for applications requiring programmable servo feedrate and advanced depth control.

Highly reliable 57 peak amp Power Supply. One amp power setting for drilling with small diameter electrodes.

Specially designed power supply cabinet to shield and cool printed circuit boards and internal electronics. Multiple power supply circuit breakers protect and extend the life of critical components.

Low-maintenance High-Pressure Dielectric Fluid Pump.

Utilize electrodes up to 18 inches (457mm) in length. Drill to depths over 300 times hole diameter with deionized water dielectric fluid.

"SOFT START" feature virtually eliminates entry point burr formation. Power settings are automatically lowered upon initial contact and then gradually increased to full power.

Automatic part referencing to reduce setup time.

THE CT400 5A IS A FIVE AXIS CNC EDM HOLE DRILLING MACHINE DESIGNED FOR COMPLEX PARTS REQUIRING HOLES AT MULTIPLE ANGLES. COMMON APPLICATIONS INCLUDE TURBINE ENGINE COMPONENTS, NOZZLE AND SPRAY APERTURES, MEDICAL PARTS AND HYDRAULIC VALVES.

FLEXIBLE MACHINE DESIGN ALLOWS THE CT400 TO BE MODIFIED FOR DIFFERENT PART SIZES. THE X-AXIS BRIDGE CAN BE RAISED WITH SPACERS FOR TALLER PARTS AND THE ROTARY TABLE CAN BE REPOSITIONED TO UTILIZE THE GRANITE WORKTABLE FOR FLAT PARTS. MOVING BRIDGE CONSTRUCTION ENABLES LONGER Y-AXIS STROKE WHILE MINIMIZING MACHINE FOOTPRINT. THE ADDED BENEFIT OF A STATIONARY WORKTABLE IS THE INCREASE IN PART WEIGHT CAPACITY.

THE SIEMENS M IS OUR MID-RANGE CNC CONTROLLER DESIGNED FOR GENERAL PRODUCTION APPLICATIONS. IT SHARES MANY OF THE SAME FEATURES AS THE 840Dsl BUT WITH A SMALLER DISPLAY. AUTOMATIC PART REFERENCING AND DRILLING CYCLES GREATLY SIMPLIFY SETUP AND PROGRAMMING. WHEN PAIRED WITH THE AEC24 AUTOMATIC ELECTRODE CHANGER, THE CT400S WILL PROVIDE MANY HOURS OF UNATTENDED PRODUCTION.

THE CT400 IS ALSO AVAILABLE IN THREE AXIS CONFIGURATION.

CT500 5A
TECHNICAL INFORMATION

The CT500S 5A is a five axis CNC EDM hole drilling machine designed for production applications. With automatic electrode changing, extensive programming tools and Fast Servo and Advanced Break Through Detection, the CT500S 5A is one of the most advanced EDM drillers available today.

The powerful Siemens 840 Dsl controller and Current EDM’s comprehensive, drilling-specific programming provides the tools you need to produce consistent, high-quality parts.

To maximize yield, the worktable enclosure is open in the front for quick part loading and unloading. Safety light curtains guard the opening ensuring safe machine operation. Scheduled maintenance is simplified with easily accessible machine components. The CT500S is also available in three axis configuration.

STANDARD MACHINE FEATURES

Rigid FIXED BRIDGE design for optimum machining accuracy and low maintenance.

X/Y/Z axis travel of 22x14x16 inch (559x356x406mm) enables the drilling of large or multiple workpieces.

High-precision, recirculating ball way slides used on X, Y and Z axes. Precision ground ball screws for optimum positioning accuracy.

Powerful Siemens 840 DsI industrial controller for complex programming.

Specially designed power supply cabinet to shield and cool printed circuit boards and other internal components. Multiple power supply circuit breakers protect and extend the life of critical components.

One amp power setting for drilling with extremely small diameter electrodes. Reset Discharge (RD) energy control improves electrode referencing accuracy and simplifies the positioning of electrodes in existing holes or cavities.

"SOFT START" feature virtually eliminates entry point burr formation. Power settings are automatically lowered upon initial contact and then gradually increased to full power.

Automatic part referencing to reduce setup time.

Precision lapped GRANITE WORKTABLE. Exceptional flatness and electrical insulation enhance machining performance.
